Check our Co-values

Accountability & Transparency

Staff should take responsible for actions and decisions and have the ability to provide sound reasoning for their actions. Activities are to be conducted in an open and clear manner.

Respect for Persons

Respect for oneself and for others to be demonstrated through good manners, courtesy, politeness, and a general awareness of their rights, wishes, concerns, and feelings.

Equal Right

Staff to be treated fairly and be given equal opportunity and rights respected regardless of rank, position, racial difference or color of skin, marital status, tribe or religion, learned or illiterate. Persons with disability should be treated with fairness w ithout discrimination.

Integrity

Staff are expected to behave ethically right in the workplace and outside the workplace for good reputation of the organization. Every staff should embrace politeness of mannerism and courtesy in behaviour or speech, treat each other with civility and respect.

Honesty

Staff should be known for telling the truth, being upright, candid and sincere with what you say and do anytime and everywhere.

Responsive Speed

Staff should be known for their proactiveness, acting fast/quick to respond to any given assignment producing timely quality results.
